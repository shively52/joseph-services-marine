About Us

Your Trusted Marine Service Partner Since 2004

Our Story

With over 20 years of experience in marine mechanics, Joseph Services Marine has been the go-to mobile marine service provider in Southwest Florida. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional marine mechanical services directly to our customers' locations.

Founded by Joseph Sierra, our company has grown from a one-man operation to a trusted team of skilled professionals, serving boat owners throughout Cape Coral, Fort Myers, and surrounding areas.

Why Choose Us?

  • Over 20 years of marine mechanical expertise
  • Mobile service - we come to your location
  • Certified and experienced technicians
  • Comprehensive diagnostic and repair services
  • Emergency repair services available
